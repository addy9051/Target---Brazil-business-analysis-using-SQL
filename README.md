# Target---Brazil-business-analysis-using-SQL
### Context:

Target is a globally renowned brand and a prominent retailer in the United States. Target makes itself a preferred shopping destination by offering outstanding value, inspiration, innovation and an exceptional guest experience that no other retailer can deliver.

This particular business case focuses on the operations of Target in Brazil and provides insightful information about 100,000 orders placed between 2016 and 2018. The dataset offers a comprehensive view of various dimensions including the order status, price, payment and freight performance, customer location, product attributes, and customer reviews.

By analyzing this extensive dataset, it becomes possible to gain valuable insights into Target's operations in Brazil. The information can shed light on various aspects of the business, such as order processing, pricing strategies, payment and shipping efficiency, customer demographics, product characteristics, and customer satisfaction levels.


## Actionable Insights 
#### For more details on each insight refer to each of the “Analysis Question” pages in Target_SQL_Project.pdf

 1. Time period of the data is from: 2016 - 2018.
 2. The customers orders were from 4119 cities and 27 states.
 3. Yearly trend of number of orders made:
	 - exponential growth of 13609% seen from 2016 to 2017.
	 - growth in the next year 2018 was only 20%.
 4. Yearly spread into new cities and states:
	 - Orders received from 3111 new cities and 6 new states in 2017 from 2016.
	 - No much spread noticed in 2018.
 5. There is also an observed growth of 20% from 2017 to 2018 although there has been a reduction in the number of cities from last year.
 6. There is no seasonality observed from this limited time period data.
 7. Afternoon time has the highest number of purchases made by customers.
 8. Customers from state SP has made the highest orders for Aug - 2018.
 9. Customers from state SP has the maximum number of customers who have ordered in the three years 2016-2018.
 10. Customers from state RR has the minimum number of customers who have ordered in the three years 2016-2018.
 11. Increase in the cost of orders is 134% in 2018 from 2017.
 12. Maximum orders between 2016-2018 have been delivered within 7 days.
 13. Customer state AL has the fourth highest average time to delivery and also the lowest difference to estimated delivery.
 14. Most common payment type used per month in 2016-2018 is credit card.
 15. Most orders are made by availing the payment installment "option 1"
 
 ## Recommendations

1. We can see a high number of active purchases made by customers from 8AM - 11PM so there is a scope to grow sales by :
	- Implementing different price-discounting strategies for the most common purchased products and product categories.
	- Making the user experience seamless by improving the website and the mobile app. There should be no lag in the payment processing whether it be made through website or the app.
2. Although there has been an increase of 134% in the cost of orders in 2018 from 2017 for months January to August there has been no significant impact on profitability, but we can impose some cost reducing policies (like discount in freight for bulk orders from sellers) to increase the profit percentage for the next year.
3. State AL has the worst delay-to-ontime ratio among all the states to improve on the delivery of products in this state we need to find what is causing the delay, maybe it is because of inefficient carrier delivery service providers in that state. If that is the case, we need to change to a better carrier to make more ontime delivery to the cities in this state.
4. Since the most common mode of payment is credit card we can make sure the payment portal for managing such processes are well maintained. We can also provide rewards based on specific cards to the customers.
